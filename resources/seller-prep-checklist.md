# Seller Pre-Listing Checklist
Grounded Hearts & Homes

## Curb Appeal
- Lawn trimmed
- Landscaping refreshed
- Entryway cleaned
- Exterior touch-up paint

## Interior Prep
- Declutter 50%
- Remove personal photos
- Deep clean entire home
- Neutralize strong colors if necessary

## Repairs
- Fix minor drywall damage
- Repair leaky faucets
- Replace burnt-out bulbs
- Service HVAC if needed

## Staging Strategy
- Simplify furniture layout
- Highlight natural light
- Define each room’s purpose

## Documentation
- Utility averages
- Age of roof/HVAC
- Recent upgrades list
- Survey (if available)

---

### Our Goal
Position your home to:
- Attract maximum attention
- Generate competitive offers
- Minimize days on market
