# Buyer Consultation Framework
Grounded Hearts & Homes

## Vision & Motivation
- What is prompting your move right now?
- What does “winning” look like in this purchase?
- Is this primarily lifestyle, investment, or necessity driven?

## Timeline
- Ideal move date?
- Flexibility range?
- Lease expiration (if applicable)?

## Financial Position
- Have you spoken with a lender?
- Pre-approved amount?
- Monthly comfort zone vs. max approval?
- Down payment amount?

## Property Preferences
- Must-haves
- Nice-to-haves
- Deal-breakers
- School district preferences?
- Commute requirements?

## Long-Term Strategy
- 3-year plan?
- 5-year plan?
- Is this a stepping stone or long-term hold?
- Rental potential important?

## Risk Tolerance
- Comfortable with renovation?
- Competitive offer strategy?
- Escalation clauses acceptable?

## Decision Process
- Who is involved in decision making?
- How quickly can you make an offer if the right property appears?
